=========================
 INTRODUCTION
=========================

Zenith is a story focused RPG game set in the fantasy, magical country of Zenith – a dictatorship ruled 
over by The Four Horsemen (Conquest, War, Famine, and Death). The main character is Halcyon - a teenage 
boy from a small village named Solaria , right off the coast of Zenith. Walk around and interact with 
the townspeople and get to know their stories!


=========================
 CONTROLS
=========================

Left Arrow       - Walk Left
Right Arrow      - Walk Right
Up Arrow         - Walk Up
Down Arrow       - Walk Down
X                - Sprint
Z                - Interact, Click Through Dialogue
P                - Pause
Esc              - Go to Main Menu
M                - Mute Music
NUM 0,1,2,3,6,7  - Dialogue
